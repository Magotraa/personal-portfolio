Research Overview

Explainable AI Systems

My research focuses on developing interpretable machine learning models that provide transparent decision-making processes. Key objectives include:

  • Creating AI systems with clear, understandable reasoning
  • Developing methods to trace AI decision paths
  • Enhancing trust in artificial intelligence technologies

Current work involves bridging the gap between complex algorithmic processes and human comprehension.

Natural Language Processing

Advanced research in linguistic computational intelligence, with a focus on:

  • Multi-lingual semantic analysis
  • Context-aware language understanding
  • Cross-cultural communication technologies

Developing innovative approaches to improve machine understanding of human language nuances.

Machine Learning in Environmental Science

Applying computational intelligence to environmental challenges:

  • Predictive modeling of climate change impacts
  • Data-driven environmental pattern recognition
  • Sustainable technology development

Leveraging advanced analytics to address critical environmental issues.
